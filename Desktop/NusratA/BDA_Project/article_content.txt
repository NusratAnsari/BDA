Arbaaz Merchant's lawyer to file fresh bail plea in session court tomorrow

After a Mumbai court rejected the bail pleas of Aryan Khan, Arbaaz Merchant and Munmun Dhamecha in cruise ship drug raids case, Arbaaz Merchant's lawyer said he will file a fresh bail plea in session court on Saturday.

Aryan Khan's lawyer Satish Maneshinde is yet to confirm if he will be filing a fresh bail plea.
